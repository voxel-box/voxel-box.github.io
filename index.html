Rules Hub

Every server has its own ruleset to keep the experience fair, immersive, and welcoming.

Voxelbox-Minecraft Rules

  • No griefing, stealing, or unauthorized base entry.
  • Use respectful chat; hate speech and spam are zero tolerance.
  • Keep farms optimized to prevent lag and avoid chunk flooding.
  • Event arenas are safe zones unless flagged otherwise.

Voxelbox-StarRupture Rules

  • Claimed outposts require permission before mining or building.
  • Coordinate large raids in faction channels to prevent friendly fire.
  • No exploitative resource duplication or physics abuse.
  • Respect PvE event timers and rotate boss loot fairly.

Voxelbox-BeamMP Rules

  • Drive responsibly in cruise zones; no intentional ramming.
  • Follow event marshal directions during races or drift nights.
  • Use appropriate vehicles for track categories.
  • Report crashes or desync issues in the support channel.

Voxelbox-SOTF Rules

  • No combat logging; stay in session until clear.
  • Raids must be announced in global chat before breaching.
  • Respect cooldowns on high-tier loot routes.
  • Solo players have a protected start period.

Voxelbox-Enshrouded Rules

  • Do not tamper with flame altars belonging to other groups.
  • Keep shared quest hubs stocked and organized.
  • Cross-party trading should be fair and transparent.
  • Keep the shroud clean of bug exploits or terrain abuse.

Voxelbox-Palworld Rules

  • No capture trapping or griefing of other players' pals.
  • Follow trading etiquette and keep markets scam-free.
  • Base territories require permission before expansion nearby.
  • Report glitches or duplicate pals to staff immediately.

Voxelbox-Satisfactory Rules

  • Shared factory zones must stay organized and labeled.
  • Coordinate mega-builds with engineering leads.
  • Power grid changes require a heads-up in ops chat.
  • No dismantling others' machines without approval.
