Rules Hub

Clear rules, better space. Respect other members, keep it welcoming, and protect the servers — full details at voxelbox.org/rules.

Minecraft — protect builds, respect shared space

  • Keep farms/redstone reasonable so they don't tank performance.
  • Ask before altering shared roads, hubs, or community projects.
  • Use mods and automation only if clearly allowed.
  • Don't block major routes, core resources, or spawn access.

Palworld — keep bases playable for everyone

  • Avoid excessive breeding/automation that creates lag.
  • Respect shared agreements on boss progression and resources.
  • Follow any posted team or guild setup.

Satisfactory — build cleanly, communicate

  • Don't rip up shared factories or train lines without checking in.
  • Label major production areas so others understand them.
  • Coordinate nuclear, power, and high-load projects.
  • Avoid abandoned half-finished builds in key areas.

Enshrouded — respect progression and shared spaces

  • Don't rush key progression without agreement.
  • Be careful with shared chests, gear, and stockpiles.
  • Keep base edits collaborative in public areas.
  • Treat boss attempts as a community activity.

American Truck Sim — drive for the convoy

  • Listen to convoy hosts and route notes.
  • Don't intentionally ram, block, or troll drivers.
  • Show up with the right mods, DLC, and setup.
  • Communicate if you need to leave a convoy.

BeamMP — keep sessions fun and readable

  • Don't intentionally ram, grief, or disrupt sessions.
  • Read the current session/event notes before joining.
  • Respect posted route, event, or host guidance.
  • Use support if your setup causes repeated issues.

FiveM — roleplay in good faith

  • Stay in character in RP zones; keep meta-gaming out of the city.
  • No RDM/VDM — violence needs a story reason.
  • Don't exploit scripts, dupe, or abuse game bugs.
  • Follow staff and posted city rules — they're the canon.
